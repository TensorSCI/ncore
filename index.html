ABOUT US

AN

FUNDRAISER

Ncore is an All India Online NEET Mock Exam conducted by Calicut Medical College Union 2020 for the current medical entrance aspirants. IMPRINTS is a charity organization run by Independents College Union in association with the Department of Pediatrics dedicated to collecting funds and supplying medical aids to children suffering from diseases such as Type 1 Diabetes, Wilson's disease, Thalassemia and other rare genetic disorders. A monthly expense of approximately 1 lakh is required for running IMPRINTS and proving aid for the numerous families we serve. These expenses used to be met by mass collection activities from the public as well as IMRPINTS fund raiser events held at the college every year. Owing to the Corona pandemic and lockdown restrictions these activities have been arrested. The current NEET aspirants lack proper exam prep and are in dire need of quality coaching from reliable experts. To clear NEET is a dream of many students & IMPRINTS is a hope to many Children . Ncore is an initiative aimed at collaborating and meeting these two needs. The Online mock test will consist of high grade questions prepared by reliable and experienced students of Calicut Medical College who have already cleared the entrance exam securing top positions. On behalf of over 80 families we serve ; we kindly request you to co-operate with this altruistic initiative.
